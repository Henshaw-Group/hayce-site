Privacy Notice

Effective: November 11, 2025 • Locale: Canada (en-CA)

We respect your privacy. This notice explains what we collect during the pre-launch waitlist, why we collect it, and how we protect your information.

What we collect

  • Contact: name, email, optional phone
  • Location: city and country
  • Preferences: platform (iOS/Android), role (Poster/Provider/Both), optional referral code
  • Optional interest signals: what you might use Hayce for and skills you could offer
  • Technical: basic device info (user agent) and timestamps

How we use it

  • To contact you with early access updates and onboarding instructions
  • To plan city rollouts and prioritize features
  • To improve sign-up experience and prevent spam/abuse

Legal basis & our commitments

  • Consent: you opt in to receive waitlist updates and can opt out anytime.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure our service (e.g., App Check / anti-abuse) and operate analytics if enabled.
  • We do not sell your data. We only share with service providers that help us run Hayce (e.g., Firebase) under appropriate safeguards.

Security & retention

We use industry-standard safeguards and platform protections (including Google Firebase security rules and App Check) to reduce misuse. We retain waitlist data until launch planning is complete or you request deletion, whichever is earlier.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your information.

Your rights & ours

We may update this notice as our products or laws evolve. If changes are material, we will let you know by email or on this page. Your continued use after changes means you accept the updated notice. Nothing in this notice limits our legal rights or defenses.
